Game Objective

Start with $10,000 in virtual money and try to grow your portfolio by buying and selling stocks. Learn how real stock markets work without any financial risk!

Game Controls

  • Start Market: Begin price updates and news events
  • Pause Market: Stop price changes while you analyze and make trades
  • Reset Game: Return to starting conditions with $10,000
  • Trading is always available: You can buy/sell stocks whether the market is running or paused

Market Shocks & Booms

  • Market Crashes: Sudden events that cause widespread price drops
  • Market Booms: Positive events that drive all stocks higher
  • Breaking News: Major events affecting individual companies
  • Economic Events: Real-world scenarios that impact the entire market

Trading Tips

  • Buy Low, Sell High: The basic principle of profitable trading
  • Watch the News: Major events can cause dramatic price changes
  • Diversify: Don't put all your money in one stock
  • Use Pause Strategically: Pause to analyze trends during volatile periods
  • React to Shocks: Market crashes and booms present opportunities
